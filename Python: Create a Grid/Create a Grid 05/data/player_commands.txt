reload inventory (rpi)
- reloads the player's inventory from
  the original file(s).
* * * *
reload player (rp)
- repopulates the player's data from
  the original file(s).
  This will reload the player's
  inventory as well.
* * * *
reload monsters (rm)
- copies all the monster data from
  the original master files into
  the player's directory.
* * * *
reload all (ra)
- resets the player AND the monsters.
